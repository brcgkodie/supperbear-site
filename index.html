Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is Supper Bear free?

Yes — Supper Bear is free to use, with no ads and no third-party advertising trackers.

What devices does it work on?

iPhone via the App Store, and any modern web browser on phones, tablets, and computers — so the whole household can use it on whatever device they have.

How do family members share a kitchen?

Each household has a simple invite code. Anyone you share it with signs in, enters the code, and sees the same meal plan, recipes, and grocery list, synced in real time.

How does "Ask about this recipe" work?

Open any recipe and tap Ask about this recipe. It already knows the full recipe, so you can ask for substitutions with amounts, scaling, spice tweaks, or a different cooking method.

How do I sign in?

Sign in with Apple, Google, or a passwordless email code — your choice. On iPhone you can unlock with Face ID.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Open the Household tab and choose "Delete account." It removes your account and your membership in any household.
