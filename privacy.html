Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BRCG Digital Partners Inc. ("BRCG," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information in connection with the Supper Bear mobile app and website (together, the "Service"). Supper Bear is a family meal-planning product. We built it to be simple and respectful of your data. We do not sell your personal information, we do not show third-party ads, and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

By using the Service, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: your email address and the first and last name you provide. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and name from that provider (and, with Sign in with Apple, you may choose to hide your email via Apple's private relay).
  • Household content: the meal plans, favorites, custom recipes, grocery selections, kitchen and pantry inventory, household name, and any photo you upload for your household. This content is shared with the members of your household who have your invite code.
  • Voice and chat input: when you use voice kitchen setup or the "ask about this recipe" feature, the audio or text you submit is processed to generate a response (see Section 3).

Information collected automatically

  • Technical and log data: standard request and security logs (such as IP address, device/browser type, and timestamps) needed to operate, secure, and debug the Service.
  • On-device storage: we store your session token and preferences locally on your device. We do not use advertising identifiers.

2. How we use your information

  • Create and secure your account and let you sign in (including Face ID unlock, which happens on your device — we never receive your biometric data).
  • Sync your household's meal plans, lists, and kitchen across members' devices.
  • Transcribe your voice into a kitchen inventory and answer your recipe questions.
  • Send you transactional messages you request (login codes and optional reminders).
  • Maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the Service, and comply with legal obligations.

Our legal bases (where GDPR/UK GDPR applies) are: performance of our contract with you, your consent (e.g., reminders, microphone access), and our legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service.

We share information only with the processors needed to run the Service, and only what each needs to perform its function. We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

4. Data retention and deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can delete your account at any time inside the app: open the Household tab and choose "Delete account." This removes your user record and your membership in any household. Shared household content that other members still rely on may remain in that household. You may also email us to request deletion or a copy of your data. We will honor verifiable requests as required by law.

6. Children's privacy

Supper Bear is intended for adults managing a household kitchen and is rated 4+. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

Sign-in is passwordless (email codes, Apple, or Google), and sessions use signed tokens. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and stored with our hosting provider with access restricted to your household via your invite code. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

8. International transfers

We are based in the United States and process information there. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., you understand your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. and other countries where our providers operate.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide additional notice where appropriate.
