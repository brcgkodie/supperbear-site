Common questions

How do my family members join?

Open Household to find your invite code. Anyone you share it with can sign in, enter the code, and they'll see the same meal plan and grocery list, synced in real time.

How do I sign in?

Use Apple, Google, or a passwordless email code — your choice. On your device you can unlock with Face ID so you don't re-enter anything.

How do I delete my account?

Open Household → Delete account. This removes your account and your membership in any household.

How does "Ask about this recipe" work?

Open any recipe and tap Ask about this recipe. It already knows the full recipe, so you can ask for substitutions, scaling, spice tweaks, or how to cook it a different way.

Reminders

You can turn the optional Sunday meal-plan reminder on or off anytime in Household → Reminders.
